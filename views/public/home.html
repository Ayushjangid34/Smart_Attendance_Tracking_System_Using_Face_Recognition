Smart Attendance Tracking System

The Smart Attendance Tracking System (SATS) is a web-based platform designed to streamline and automate the management of attendance data in educational institutions. The system allows teachers, students, and administrators to easily track, manage, and access attendance records.

The SATS system enables the collection of attendance data for each class, including student presence, subject details, faculty, and class timings. It ensures that accurate records are maintained for every student and every class session.

One of the key features of SATS is that attendance information is securely accessible to registered students, teachers, and administrators. This ensures transparency and allows stakeholders to stay updated on student participation and class schedules.
